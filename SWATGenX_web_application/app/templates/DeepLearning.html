Deep Learning Models for Predicting Groundwater Hydraulic Properties

Our deep learning models leverage state-of-the-art techniques to provide accurate predictions of groundwater hydraulic properties, helping researchers and engineers make informed decisions.

Sparse Fully Convolutional Network Classification

The Sparse Fully Convolutional Network (Sparse FCN) model is designed to classify and predict hydraulic conductivity with high precision. It efficiently handles sparse data, making it ideal for large-scale groundwater modeling.

ResNet Deep Learning Prediction

The ResNet model applies deep residual learning to predict groundwater hydraulic properties. It captures complex patterns and relationships within the data, ensuring robust and accurate predictions.
