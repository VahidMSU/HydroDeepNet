Highlights

  • MODFLOW_250m/: 5-layer MODFLOW model at 250m resolution.
  • SWAT_MODEL/: Built with high-resolution datasets including NHDPlus HR and NLCD.
  • SWAT_gwflow_MODEL/: Calibrated using PSO with optimization for multiple parameters:
    • Objective function includes NSE for streamflow, ET, GW, SWL, and Yield.
    • Weights: 1 for streamflow, SWL, GW; 0.1 for ET and Yield.
    • Simplified GWFlow model with 1 layer (compared to 5-layer MODFLOW).
  • Streamflow Data: Observed data in `streamflow_data/` directory.
  • Calibration Results: Includes last 100 outputs for daily and monthly simulations.

Important Notes

The directory also includes static plots, animations, and detailed calibration results. Users may need to fine-tune the models for specific requirements. For additional scenarios, explore the `SWAT_gwflow_MODEL/Scenarios` folder.
